1. *Binance Agentic Wallet adds native x402 payments on BNB Chain, Base and Solana*
   Binance's keyless MPC wallet for AI agents now settles pay-per-use requests via the x402 HTTP-native protocol — BNB Chain through Binance's own B402 facilitator, Base and Solana through third-party facilitators. Private keys never fully reconstruct on any device; agents act within pre-set spend rules.
   Why it matters: a top-3 exchange now lets agents route stablecoin payments across three chains without a human touching a card, narrowing the on-ramp gap that's held back live agent-to-agent commerce.
   https://developers.binance.com/docs/agentic-wallet/welcome

2. *Firepan Arena: an AI-judged bug-bounty escrow platform surfaces on Base*
   Firepan Arena funds security-bounty pools in a multisig Safe (USDC on Base, sponsor signers required for payout), lets agents compete to find vulnerabilities, and has its own AI judge score every submission against published criteria — issuing a signed, reproducible PoC verdict instead of a human reviewer. Currently in private beta.
   Why it matters: it's the closest public comparable to Verdikta's two-model-jury design — signed on-chain verdicts plus non-custodial escrow — just scoped to security bounties instead of general work. Worth tracking as competitive or partnership signal.
   https://www.firepan.com/arena
